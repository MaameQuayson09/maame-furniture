Our Story

Blending Ghanaian craftsmanship with cutting-edge 3D technology

Celebrating Ghanaian Craftsmanship

AfroFurnish was founded in 2018 with a mission to bring Ghana's rich furniture-making heritage into the digital age. Our team combines generations of woodworking expertise with modern 3D visualization technology to create pieces that honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Each piece in our collection is designed in Ghana by our team of local artisans and designers. We work with sustainably sourced hardwoods from Ghana's forests, ensuring both quality and environmental responsibility.

Our Craftsmanship Process

From forest to finished product with Ghanaian care

1

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner with certified Ghanaian forestry operations to source mahogany, odum, and other premium hardwoods.

2

Traditional Design

Our designers draw inspiration from Ghana's rich cultural heritage, incorporating Adinkra symbols and traditional motifs.

3

Artisan Crafting

Skilled craftsmen in our Kumasi workshop hand-shape each piece using both traditional and modern techniques.

4

Quality Finishing

Each piece receives multiple layers of eco-friendly finish for protection and to enhance the natural wood grain.

Sustainable Practices

At AfroFurnish, we're committed to preserving Ghana's forests for future generations:

  • For every tree used, we plant three new seedlings through our partnership with Ghana Forestry Commission
  • All wood scraps are recycled into smaller items or used for workshop fuel
  • We use water-based, non-toxic finishes that are safe for artisans and customers
  • Our workshop runs on 60% solar power, reducing our carbon footprint
