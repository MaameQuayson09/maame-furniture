Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Payment

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) through our secure payment gateway.

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Custom orders may take 3-5 business days for processing before shipping.

Shipping & Delivery

We deliver nationwide! Delivery to Accra, Kumasi, and Takoradi is free for orders over GHS 2,000. Other regions may incur additional delivery charges.

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email that you can use to monitor your delivery status.
