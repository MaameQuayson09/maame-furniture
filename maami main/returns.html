Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return policy and simple process for exchanges.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your item, we're happy to offer returns or exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

30-Day Window

You have 30 days from the delivery date to initiate a return or exchange.

Original Condition

Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and with all tags attached.

Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Custom or personalized items
  • Final sale items (marked as such on product page)
  • Items damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear
  • Gift cards

How to Return an Item

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account and go to "Order History" to start the return process. Alternatively, contact our customer service team with your order number.

Pack Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags and accessories included. Include the original packing slip or invoice.

Ship It Back

Use the prepaid return label we provide (for US returns) or ship the package to our return center. For international returns, you may need to cover return shipping costs.

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Exchange Process

Want a different size, color, or style? Here's how exchanges work:

Same Product Exchanges

For exchanges of the same product in a different size or color, follow the return process and place a new order for the item you want.

Different Product Exchanges

For exchanges to a completely different product, return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired product.

Quick Exchange Service

For eligible items, we offer a Quick Exchange service where we ship your new item as soon as your return is initiated, before we receive your returned item.
